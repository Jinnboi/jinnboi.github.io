About Me

Hello! My name is Marcos Mancia, and I was born and raised in Southern California. Being one of four children growing up, I was constantly surrounded by energy, creativity, and a spirit of collaboration. This (tad bit chaotic) environment fostered my passion for problem-solving and innovation, ultimately leading me to pursue the field of Computer Engineering at Columbia University! My academic journey thus far has been both challenging and incredibly exciting, providing me with a deep understanding of computer systems, software development, and my favorite, electronics. At Columbia specifically, I’ve had the amazing opportunity to work on a variety of intriguing projects, from designing complex circuits for signal processing to developing programs that implement data structures from scratch. These experiences together sparked a keen interest within me in the subfield of Embedded Systems! Outside of my academics, I spend much of my time pursuing my faith as a Christian. My belief is an integral part of my identity, guiding my values and driving me to make meaningful contributions to my community and society as a whole. It provides me with a strong moral foundation and motivates me to give my best effort in all of my endeavors for the glory of God. As I look ahead to the future, I am enthusiastic about the unique opportunities that will arise. Much love and respect, and God bless!
